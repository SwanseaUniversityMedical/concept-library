About HDRN Canada

HDRN Canada is a non-profit organization that connects individuals and organizations from across the country to share expertise, identify opportunities for collaboration, and foster innovation in health data research. Our goal is to improve health and health equity by supporting transformative and world-leading multi-regional health data use.

Data Access Support Hub (DASH)

Our Data Access Support Hub (DASH) serves as a single point of access and information portal for multi-regional health data. DASH supports researchers through various stages of the data access process to ensure a seamless experience.

Our Initiatives

We are involved in several initiatives aimed at improving access to health data and supporting health data research across Canada.

For the Public

We engage with members of the public in decisions about health data and provide resources to answer your questions about health data collection, storage, access, and use.
